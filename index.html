The Fight of my Husband’s Life

My name is Taruna Jignesh Vasa, and I am raising funds for my husband, Mr. Jignesh P Vasa, who a 50-year-old loving father of 2 teenage daughters, a son to an ageing mother (75 years old), and my husband, who is bravely fighting oral cancer for the fourth time.

His journey has been nothing short of struggles — but also inspiring.

More than A Decade of Battle

Jignesh was first diagnosed with oral cancer in 2013. He underwent surgery, 6 cycles of chemotherapy, and 35 cycles of radiation, and got cured. But the cancer came back just a couple of years later, in 2015. Once again, he went through the ordeal of surgery and aggressive treatments followed by another 6 cycles of chemotherapy, and 35 cycles of radiation. The side effects were devastating.

Despite it all, he kept going. For his Family. For his Daughters. For his Wife. For Life.

But in 2024, cancer returned for the third time. Doctors couldn’t offer him more radiation or any such harsh treatment — his body had endured all it could. He underwent a third surgery along with oral reconstruction - that left him barely able to eat and speak — he lost the ability to eat solid food and he could not speak normally.

And now, in 2025, our worst nightmare is unfolding once more: the cancer has returned for the fourth time.

His Hope Now: Immunotherapy plus concurrent Chemotherapy - for the next couple of years or more.

This time, the viable treatment option seems to be immunotherapy along with Concurrent Chemotherapy. It’s his only chance at life. Plus we are also exploring Homeopathy and Government approved Ayush Treatment.

The Immunotherapy and concurrent Chemotherapy treatment has begun since 25th April 2025, and will cost us approximately INR ₹ 20,00,000 (US $ 22,000 approx.) over the period of next 2 years. This estimated cost includes Immunotherapy Charges, Chemotherapy Charges, Hospital Costs, Doctors Charges, Medications, Hospital Visits, Nutritional Costs, Home Rent, a few other mandatory family expenses, etc.

Unfortunately, Jignesh has exhausted every rupee of savings we had, including his Provident Fund money, over the last 10 years due to repeated surgeries, hospital stays, treatments, medications, family expenses, daughters' education, mother's medical expenses, and house rent. We are currently out of financial options — but not out of hope.

Why We Need Your Help

Jignesh has been the sole breadwinner for his family of 5 including his ageing mother, wife and 2 teenage daughters. But he lost his job in August 2022 because his health did not support him to work efficiently.

Since then, Jignesh has been trying to support us through some Stock Trading. But his health decline has made even that impossible.

We live in a rented apartment, and we are unable to meet the Treatment Expenses & the Family’s other mandatory monthly expenses.

Therefore, we need your help in donating generously to help Jignesh and our family survive through this testing time. We’re not just asking for money — we’re asking for a chance at life. Your donation will be used towards his Immunotherapy Charges, Chemotherapy Charges, Hospital Costs, Doctors Charges, Medications, Hospital Visits, Nutritional Costs, Home Rent, a few other mandatory Family Expenses, etc.
